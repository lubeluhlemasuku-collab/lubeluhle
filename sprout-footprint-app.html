Meet Sprout

Sprout is a little forest spirit whose health mirrors your household's footprint. Answer a few honest questions about your water, power, travel and daily habits — real South African numbers do the maths, Sprout just tells the story. No guessing, just growing.

Water

Every litre took energy to treat and pump to your tap. Got a bill handy? Even better — exact beats estimated.
Check your municipal bill — 1 kL = 1,000 litres

Electricity

South Africa's grid is still coal-heavy, so kWh here carry more CO₂ than in most countries. This is usually the biggest lever you have.

Backup power & loadshedding

Generators burn fuel directly, so they can spike a footprint fast. Solar and grid-charged batteries are far gentler.
